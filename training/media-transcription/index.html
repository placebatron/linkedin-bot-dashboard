Good to know

A few rules & gotchas

  • Baum recordings only. It's an internal tool — use your work files.
  • 3+ speakers? The transcript is complete, but the "who said it" labels can get muddy — read those with a skeptical eye.
  • Not a legal record. It's very good, not perfect. Check the recording before you quote anyone.
  • Duplicates are skipped. Drop the same file twice and Corey notices — no double transcripts.

⚖️ Consent matters — Illinois is two-party consent. Everyone on a call must know it's being recorded. If everyone knew, you're fine. If you're not sure, ask Mike before recording — not after.

Privacy & retention

Who sees it, and for how long

The transcript is the record we keep; the raw recording is kept only as long as needed:

📄 Transcripts stay in Notion. Your transcript lives in the Notion archive it's filed under — it is not deleted.

🗑️ Recordings auto-delete after 10 days. The original audio file is removed automatically — we don't store recordings longer than necessary.

🔒 Limited visibility. While a recording still exists, its backup is visible only to David and Mike.

When in doubt about a sensitive recording, ask Mike before dropping it in.
