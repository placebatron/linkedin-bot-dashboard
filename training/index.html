3.2 — Contacts: The Process

Meet someone? Email Geri.

Send contact info to Geri@baumrealty.com as close to real-time as possible. Don't wait for a stack of 50 cards.

  • Checks for duplicates → creates or updates
  • Enriches via third-party data (People Data Labs coming)
  • Ties contact to properties, reps & groups
  • Marks private if it's from an NDA/confidential source

This isn't residential

Contacts are shared firm-wide by default. No value in hoarding — we can always filter by creator to show "your" contacts. You can mark things private, but nobody does, and we discourage it.

Geri's instincts for dedup & enrichment beat any automated tool.

3.2 — Sales Volumes on the Map

Stand on the sidewalk. Pull the numbers.

From your laptop or phone in front of a property, pull every sales volume we have nearby. Retailers love this. Landlords love this.

Accuracy hierarchy:

  • Direct from a general manager — best
  • Offering memorandum
  • Restaurant Trends — directional, ±10%

🚫 We do NOT share this data externally. Everyone asks. The answer is always no.

NDA-sourced volume? Tell Geri to mark it private — still in your reports, just not visible to others.

Section Ten

Document Hygiene

BrokerAdmin / Acctg

10.1 / 10.2 — Track Changes & Metadata

The mistake that's bitten us before

📝 Track Changes

For LOIs, listing agreements & deal docs. Review ▸ Track Changes. Everything becomes a redline; accept/reject individually or all at once.

Be careful accepting changes to spaces, formatting, or dates — easy to approve something unintended.

⚠️ Metadata is a real risk

Every Word doc carries hidden author, company, comments & revision history. Reuse an LOI and the metadata may still name another broker — or a competitor. This has happened.

Before sending externally: File ▸ Info ▸ Check for Issues ▸ Inspect Document. Remove properties & personal info. Keep headers/footers/watermarks.
